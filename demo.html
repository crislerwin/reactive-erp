Sidebar Demo - Fixed Icons Issue

This demo shows the fixed sidebar where:

  • Before fix: When sidebar was closed, only text parts of menu items were visible instead of icons
  • After fix: When sidebar is closed, proper icons are displayed centered in the collapsed sidebar

Click the toggle button (top-left of sidebar) to see the difference!

Technical Fix Applied:

In MenuClosedItems component, replaced {item.icon} with <CustomIcon className="h-4 w-4" iconName={icon} /> to properly render Tabler icons instead of displaying icon names as text.
